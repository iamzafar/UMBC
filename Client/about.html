Welcome to the Barking Bazaar!


The Barking Bazaar is a web application designed to allow trading and bartering by the UMBC community. It was designed and created for the Fall 2016 Software Engineering class by the group of Zafar Mamarakhimov, Drake Gao, Daniel Eppinger, Nick Mitsos, and Vaughn Jackson. Thank you for using our application, and we hope you will enjoy your experience. Enjoy!
